The Clinical Advantage

Your Results Deserve
More Than a Dashboard

Online wellness testing gives you data. Physician-ordered testing gives you answers — and a plan of action.

Expert Clinical Review
Your physician personally reviews every result, identifies patterns across biomarkers, and translates data into actionable medical guidance — not just a color-coded PDF.
Seamless Continuity of Care
Abnormal findings trigger immediate follow-up testing, specialist referrals, and treatment adjustments — all within your existing care relationship. No fragmented data. No starting over.
Integrated Medical Record
Results live in your permanent health record — accessible to every provider on your care team. Online platforms create data silos. Your physician's order keeps everything connected.

Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

We've answered the most common questions below. For anything else, your physician or our team is here to help.

The Vitality Health Panel is one of the most comprehensive wellness assessments available, covering over 40 advanced tests and 120+ biomarkers across 19 health categories including heart health, thyroid function, metabolic health, brain and cognitive markers, nutrients, hormones, liver and kidney function, immune and autoimmune screening, cancer screening (men), bone health, and environmental toxin exposure. Gender-specific panels ensure testing is tailored to your needs.
The Vitality Health Panel is $399, which includes all testing, the blood draw, and any necessary reflex testing. There are no surprise fees or add-on charges. This is comparable in price and depth to well-known online wellness platforms, with the added advantage of your results becoming part of your permanent medical record.
Yes, fasting for 10–12 hours before your blood draw is required for the most accurate results, particularly for metabolic and lipid markers. Be sure to stay well-hydrated by drinking plenty of water. Your physician's office will provide specific preparation instructions.
Most results are available within 10–12 business days. Some specialized tests, such as heavy metal analysis, may require additional processing time. Your physician will contact you to review your complete results as soon as they're available.
When your physician orders the Vitality Panel, your results are reviewed by a licensed clinician who knows your history. Abnormal findings trigger immediate clinical follow-up — not a suggestion to "consult a doctor." Your results become part of your permanent health record, accessible to any specialist on your care team.
Many patients benefit from annual comprehensive wellness testing to track trends over time and catch changes early. Your physician will recommend the ideal frequency based on your health profile and goals.
The Vitality Health Panel is a self-pay wellness program priced at $399. This straightforward pricing eliminates insurance complexities and ensures you receive the full panel of tests. Your office visit with your physician is a separate charge and is typically covered by insurance. Speak with your physician about whether any individual components may be covered under your plan.
